Specifications

Prescription

With Prescription SmartLens, a regular eyeglass wearer will be able to see as well as use the SmartLens. The Prescription has all of the features of the Original SmartLens, including:

  • 8MP Camera
  • 64 GB Capacity
  • 802.11ac Wi-Fi
  • Bluetooth 5.0
  • 4G LTE

Original

The Original SmartLens is for those who do not require a prescription lens. It includes:

  • 8MP Camera
  • 64 GB Capacity
  • 802.11ac Wi-Fi
  • Bluetooth 5.0
  • 4G LTE
